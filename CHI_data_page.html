    The Windy City

    Even though Chicago's famous nickname is because of its politicians, Chicago criminals are easily swayed by the weather as well. Chicago has the strongest correlation between crime and temperature out of any of the cities we used in our study.

    Conclusion

    It looks like and open and shut case for the city of Chicago. When it is warm criminals are on the prowl and in the lake affect winters they hibernate. If we had to use just one city for our study Chicago would be a great choice because it has the largest spread of temperatures in a given year.
