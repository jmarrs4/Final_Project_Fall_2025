    Crime and the Weather

    Statisticians across the world have believed that there is a positive correlation between the weather and crime rates. We wanted to do our own research into this phenomenon and are using this website to share our findings. We took crime and weather data from the three largest cities in the US and have found strong correlations between different weather statistics and crime.

    These first three maps show us every crime we have on file as well as how the different precincts and districts are split up.

    Heated Tempers?

    This graph is at the heart of our research, and it completely goes against everything we have been told about crime and the weather. However, looking deeper into our data sets and the individual cities tells a different story that is closer to the original findings.
