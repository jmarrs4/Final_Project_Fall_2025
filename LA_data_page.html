    La La Land

    You hop off the plane at LAX with a dream and your cardigan. The first thing you notice is that the weather is nice year round! Sometimes it does not feel fair to people living in the other major US cities that go through extreme winters, but Souther California's mild weather makes itself known in the crime data. There are almost no correlations between crime and any type of weather, partially, because their really is only one type of weather.

    Crime Severity in LA

    When separated into violent and non violent crimes there is no correlation between the temperature and crime in Los Angeles. Our hypothesis is that since the temperature is much more static in Los Angeles than either of our other cities, there was not enough fluctuation in temperature to make a meaningful difference in crime.

    Conclusions

    The mild weather of Southern California completely shifts the overall crime data for our entire original data set. The consistant weather in Los Angeles midigated any effects that Chicago or New York could have had on our data because there is a such a small sample size of bad weather. I guess when it is always beutiful out there is no reason to commit crimes.
